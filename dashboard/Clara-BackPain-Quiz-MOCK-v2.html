Do you have any of these health conditions?

Select all that apply.

Why we askSome pain medications interact with certain conditions. Anti-inflammatories can affect the kidneys, stomach, heart, and blood pressure, so we hold them when those flags are present; the nerve pain medication we sometimes use (duloxetine) needs extra care with the liver and with mood conditions like bipolar disorder. This is the same safety check any prescriber would do, and it's how we keep you safe.
Kidney disease or reduced kidney function
