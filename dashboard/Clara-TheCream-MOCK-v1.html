Pills versus cream

It works where it hurts.

An oral pill

  • Travels through your whole body to reach one joint
  • More medicine in your bloodstream, more whole body side effects
  • Swallowed daily, when some would rather not

The clara cream

  • Applied right where the pain is
  • A fraction of the medicine reaches your bloodstream
  • A ladder of strengths, matched to your condition
