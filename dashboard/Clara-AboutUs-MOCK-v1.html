Discover our journey.

How three clinicians built the chronic pain platform they always wished they could send their patients home with.

We created clara because of what we kept seeing in the clinic.

Patients waiting months for chronic pain care, or never offered it at all, left to cope with a bottle and no real explanation. We wanted to change that: to bring quality chronic pain care to the people going without it, and to bring it with clarity.

So we built clara into a comprehensive chronic pain program, not a single prescription: real assessment, evidence based treatment, education, and a care team that stays with you. And we built it to reach you wherever you are.

Quality chronic pain care should never depend on how close you live to a specialist, so clara delivers it from the comfort of home, to patients in major cities and remote communities alike.

10,000+ Clinic Visits
with chronic pain patients, in person, by our founding doctor. That hard won experience is built into clara, ready for every patient from the very first visit.
Our story

Built by the people who treat pain.

clara was not built by a marketing team that found a category. It was built by three clinicians who spent their careers watching chronic pain get rushed, undertreated, and misunderstood, and decided to build something better.

We picked conditions where the evidence is real, knee osteoarthritis, low back pain, migraine, and nerve pain, and built each one properly: confirm the diagnosis, screen for danger, explain it in plain language, treat it to the evidence, and stay with you while it works.
