Please see a doctor in person first

You told us about a symptom that should be checked face-to-face before starting an online exercise plan. This is the safe, careful thing to do, most of the time it turns out fine.

Go to an emergency room now if you have:

  • Loss of bladder or bowel control, or numbness in the saddle area
  • Sudden, worsening leg weakness

For the other items, book with your family doctor or a walk-in clinic soon. clara is built for routine, non-emergency back pain, the other 10% deserves in-person attention.

This pattern finder is an educational starting point based on the Saskatchewan Provincial Spine Pathway, not a diagnosis. Your clara doctor and physiotherapist confirm your pattern and personalise your plan. If new leg weakness, numbness in the saddle area, or bladder or bowel changes appear at any time, stop and seek urgent care.
