Treating the pain itself

Why a compound cream?

Your exercises do the heavy lifting. A compound cream applied right at the knee is the teammate that makes the work more comfortable: relief comparable to pills, with far less medicine reaching the rest of your body.

  • ✓  Works alongside your movement plan, not instead of it
  • ✓  Recommended by major guidelines before oral medication
  • ✓  Applied where it hurts, gentler on stomach, heart, and kidneys

How to use this cream

Apply a thin layer to the right knee, 2 to 3 times a day. Wash hands after applying. Do not put on broken skin, cuts, or wounds. Let it dry before clothing touches it.

Give it a fair 4 to 6 week trial. Most people get partial relief, not zero pain.

How to take it

Take one 500 mg tablet by mouth twice a day, with food, for as long as your physician recommends. Avoid taking on an empty stomach.

Stop and message Clara if you have stomach pain, dark stools, or unusual bruising.
