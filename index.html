How to use this cream

Apply a thin layer to the right knee, 2 to 3 times a day. Wash hands after applying. Do not put on broken skin, cuts, or wounds. Let it dry before clothing touches it.

Give it a fair 4 to 6 week trial. Most people get partial relief, not zero pain.

Hi Dr. Nguyen, quick question. Should I take the naproxen with breakfast or wait until after my walk?
You · 3:42 PM
Great question, Sarah. Yes, taking naproxen with breakfast is fine. The food helps protect your stomach from irritation, which is the main concern with anti-inflammatories like naproxen.
If you can take it about 30 minutes before your walk, that timing is ideal. The medication will be at peak effect by the time you finish walking, which is when most people notice the most knee discomfort.
